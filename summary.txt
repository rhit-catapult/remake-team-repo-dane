Our games are called Chilling Echoes. They are made with Python. Players can choose two kinds of games. One is 3D and another one is 2D. The goal of both games is to escape from the maze. In 2D  game, players have to escape from the basement on a snowy day. But it's not like other traditional chasing games, players have the opportunity to fight back. They can either use gun to shoot at monsters or use the flashlight to stun monsters. For 3D game, those smiling faces will chase players in an indoor space. TO enter into next level, players must find a yellow exit. In the 3D game, there are various smiling face, players need to take different strategies while facing them.
